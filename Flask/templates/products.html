Introducing EcoChoice

At Walmart, we are committed to making more environmentally-friendly choices accessible to you. Our EcoChoice tag helps you easily identify products that meet our high standards for sustainability and eco-friendliness. Products labeled with the EcoChoice tag have been rigorously evaluated based on key environmental factors. These include the amount of packaging, carbon footprint, recyclability, biodegradability, and more. When you see the EcoChoice label, you can be confident that the product has been selected with care to support a greener planet.
